Founder Identity

I am the solo founder of Built From Curiosity, a learning company dedicated to redefining how technical knowledge is acquired and mastered. Driven by relentless curiosity and hands-on execution, I combine advanced technical training with modern AI-enabled development. I don't just study systems — I build them, bridge them, and optimize them to help others unlock their potential.

What She Built

I founded Built From Curiosity as the company, philosophy, and operating system behind transformative technical education. CompTIA Quest is its first product — a gamified, scenario-based iOS application designed to make CompTIA A+ certification prep active rather than passive. Developed using Base44, Codex, GitHub, and AI orchestration, the app translates dense technical curriculum into immersive, playable challenges.

Why She Built It

"I didn't start CompTIA Quest because I wanted to build an app. I built it because I kept thinking there had to be a better way."

Traditional certification prep is often dry, static, and disconnected from the real-world scenarios technicians actually face. I built this platform to prove that technical skills can be cultivated through immersive play — transforming passive studying into an active pursuit of capability.
