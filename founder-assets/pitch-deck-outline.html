SLIDE 01

CompTIA Quest: Building Real Technical Capability Through Gamified, Scenario-Based Learning

  • Mission: Help people become what they once thought was out of reach.
  • Live on the iOS App Store.
  • Product built by Built From Curiosity | Founder: Tiera Rider.
Speaker Note "We created CompTIA Quest because we believe technical capability shouldn't be gated by passive reading — it should be unlocked through active, scenario-based play. It represents the first product proof-of-concept built under our Built From Curiosity operating philosophy."
SLIDE 02

Passive Study Models Fail the Modern, Diverse IT Learner

  • Standard IT training relies on dense textbooks, video lectures, and flat practice exams.
  • Memorizing terminology ≠ knowing how to troubleshoot a real machine.
  • Non-traditional learners often lose confidence before they ever touch a simulated problem.
Speaker Note "When students are met with dry, passive slides, they don't just lose interest — they lose momentum. By shifting to active, mobile-first learning, we support learner engagement, confidence, practice consistency, and certification readiness."
SLIDE 03

Traditional Training Tools Are Gates, Not Bridges

  • Inaccessible interfaces — desktop-only labs that exclude mobile-first learners.
  • High-friction barriers — complex sign-ups before a student can try a single lesson.
  • No emotional connection — learning treated as a transaction, not a transformation.
Speaker Note "We wanted to build a bridge, not a gate, on the device your students already use most."
SLIDE 04

Active, Scenario-Based Troubleshooting in the Palm of Your Hand

  • Mobile-first gamification — scenario-driven iOS app for CompTIA A+ prep.
  • Learning by doing — students resolve real-world hardware scenarios, not definition lists.
  • Guided by a 10-principle Product Constitution established by Built From Curiosity.
Speaker Note "CompTIA Quest turns dry exam objectives into active troubleshooting missions."
SLIDE 05

The 8-Step Guest-First Learning Experience

  • Step 1–2: Download + enter as guest — zero friction.
  • Step 3–5: Laptop Components → 6 questions → first capability proof.
  • Step 6–8: Desktop Components → 5 questions → second capability proof.
  • Sign-in to save progress — In Progress.
Speaker Note "A student can download our app and, within seconds, be solving hardware scenarios — without filling out a registration form first. The guest path is fully playable today."
